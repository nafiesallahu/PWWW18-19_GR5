About us

We believe we are all students of life for life and that the whole wide world is our classroom.

FOUNDER & CEO

Alicia Williams

Alicia Williams has been a leader in education for over 15 years. She started in the sector as an elementary school teacher. During her teaching years, she received her Masters of Education in Curriculum and Instruction and wrote curriculum and assessments for the Osborn School District. Over the last decade, she has been in a variety of education leadership positions including managing the Teaching and Learning Team nationally for Teach For America, serving as a Vice President on their Teacher Preparation Support and Development Team, and leading the school design of a new innovative elementary school model with Achievement First Greenfield. She know is the principal of MS and has been focused on improving education for our students.



It is our ultimate goal to maintain an environment where parents have a piece of mind when the children are in our care. It is important to know that children are safe, happy and well taken care of. The essence of our care revolves around “The Whole Child” approach where proper education, nurture, nutrition and play combine. Our campuses and staff are continuously improving, our goal is to provide the best care for the little ones.

CARING

The philosophy at Ms is based in the belief that each child is a unique individual. Our goal is to help children feel confident and comfortable in relating needs and feelings to others and to have positive self-esteem.

COMMUNICATION

We strive to provide a warm and nurturing environment that will bring out each child’s full potential and still encourage freedom of choice. The well-being of children is the most important. Each day you will receive a full report about your child (paper or email) with full detail about your child’s day

OPEN-DOOR-POLICY

We believe that it is very important to feel safe and comfortable about your little one being away from home. That is why all of our campuses offer “open door policy” – you are ALWAYS welcome to come by or to call us as often as you feel necessary.

ENGAGING

Modern School’s interactive environment encourages curiosity, problem solving, creativity and social growth. We use positive reinforcements in all aspects of our program.

RESPECTFUL

We work together with you as a team to meet your needs and concerns in a respectful and ethical manner. Open and honest communication is the key to a successful preschool experience for children and their parents.

HELPFUL

Our friendly staff and administrators will help with every step of transition to ensure happiness and safety of the children. You will get know all teachers on campus and soon you will feel like part of our big MS family

NUTRITION

Proper nutrition gives our bodies the right amount of energy, enough raw materials and all of the “little helpers” we need to stay healthy.We provide 3 meals a day – morning snack ( or breakfast), hot lunch and afternoon snack which are included with tuition

OUR TEACHERS

Our Teachers are loving, nurturing and very caring. Transition for most children is fast and smooth. Most of our staff have been teaching at Modern School for a while, our focus is to create stability for the children.

ONGOING-EVALUATION

Our school uses assessment data to compare our children with others from across the country. Effective use of assessment data allows our school to identify problematic areas of learning at the classroom and school levels, so that teachers can generate solutions to address the problems.
