Printable Connect the Dots for Kids

Kids of all ages seem to love connecting dots to reveal a picture and they'll get to do just that in this printable activity for kids.
There are easy, intermediate, and difficult connect the dots puzzles here that include connecting numbers and the alphabet.
If you have an older child that needs more of a challenge, there are even connect the dots worksheets that have them skip count to reveal the picture.

Printable Color by Number for Kids

A step up from coloring sheets, these printable color by numbers will have your children matching and using a legend to color in a picture.
Besides regular color by number printables, there are also color by numbers that require the kids to perform some type of calculation to read the key.
These are really great for older kids who crave that creativity in each subject they study.

Seasonal Printables for Kids

Kids love to celebrate holidays and seasons, and they'll get to do just that with these seasonal printables.
